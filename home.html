What is TEN?

TEN is an opportunity for elementary to high school students to have fun with technology. Students can also learn about the possibilities of technology and engineering based careers.

The event also seeks to inform parents about how these fields can be beneficial to their child for more than just a career.

Learning about technology can foster possible life skills that will help them in any field they choose.

The evening will also showcase projects by LPS students from Project Lead The Way(PLTW) and Technology Student Association (TSA)
